Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 23, 2026

1. Overview

Anant Impex ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Anant Impex mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy informs you of our protocols regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you utilize our digital services and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

While using our application, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). Personally identifiable information explicitly includes, but is not limited to:

  • First name and last name / Company Name
  • Email address
  • Custom business requirements and trade inquiries

3. Data Processing via Third-Party Handlers

Our application handles submission data using a secure third-party form broker framework (**Formspree**). When you transmit a trade inquiry form:

  • Your entry details are safely tunneled through Formspree's encrypted structural APIs.
  • The data is delivered directly to our managed corporate communication endpoints strictly for transaction evaluation.
  • We do not sell, rent, lease, or distribute your identity markers to advertising systems.

4. Device Permissions (WebView Context)

This application operates as a secure wrapper for our responsive domain. It requests basic Internet Access Permissions to dynamically load assets and process form actions. It does not actively track user device location parameters, scan hardware storage blocks, or access device contact lists.

5. Data Security

The security of your commercial data is paramount. We implement standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL/TLS) transport protocols provided through our hosting systems. However, remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding these privacy directives, please contact us through our established platform infrastructure.
